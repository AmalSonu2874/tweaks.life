Welcome to Tweaks, the ultimate destination for CVV University students seeking premium PG services and class notes all in one place. Founded in 2025, Tweaks was created with a singular vision: to make student life easier, more organized, and stress-free by providing accurate, up-to-date listings of Paying Guest (PG) accommodations exclusively near CVV University.

At Tweaks, we understand that finding the perfect PG can be a challenge. That’s why we offer a carefully curated list of the best PG services in the vicinity of CVV University, ensuring that students get verified, comfortable, and well-reviewed options that suit their needs. Whether you’re looking for a budget-friendly stay, a premium living space, or something in between, Tweaks provides detailed listings, including amenities, pricing, locations, and contact details—all in one place.

But we don’t stop there. We know that academic success is just as important as finding the right accommodation. That’s why Tweaks also includes a dedicated section for class notes, tailored exclusively for CVV University students. We aim to support students by offering access to organized, high-quality study materials, helping them excel in their courses without hassle.


  • Exclusive to CVV University – We focus only on PG services and class notes for CVV students, making our platform highly relevant and tailored to your needs.
  • Premium & Verified PG Listings – Every PG featured on our platform meets our quality standards, ensuring a safe and comfortable stay.
  • Up-to-date & Reliable Information – No outdated or misleading listings; we ensure real-time accuracy.
  • Simplified Student Life – From finding the perfect PG to accessing class notes, we streamline everything in one premium platform.

    • At Tweaks, we don’t own, manage, or run any PG accommodations; our mission is simply to connect students with the best available PG options and provide them with valuable academic resources.

    We are committed to transparency, reliability, and convenience—because we believe that students deserve the best start to their academic journey. Whether you’re a newcomer looking for the perfect place to stay or a student in need of quick access to class notes, Tweaks is your trusted companion at CVV University.
